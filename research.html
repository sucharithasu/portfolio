Research areas, projects & ongoing work.

Five focused research lines, with active 2025–26 projects in cross-modal retrieval, medical imaging and generative augmentation — all connected by a common thread: practical, peer-reviewed AI.

Research Areas

Five lines of inquiry.

Area 01

Computer Vision & Image Retrieval

Local-pattern descriptors (LBP variants, edge binary patterns), Zernike moments and dictionary-clustering — for unsupervised content-based image retrieval in texture, face and biomedical domains.

Medical Image Analysis

Generative AI & Augmentation

Research Projects

Selected projects — objectives, methods, results.

2025 · Scientific Reports Q1

Cross-Modal Image-Text Retrieval via Dynamic Graph Networks

Objective. Replace static cross-modal attention with graph networks that model mutual context between regions and tokens.

Method. Dynamic graph construction with relation-guided message passing across image and text nodes.

Result. Outperforms attention-only baselines on standard image-text benchmarks; published in Scientific Reports.

2024 · Multimedia Tools & Applications Q1

GIEnsemformerCADx — Ensemble Formers for GI Cancer Recognition

Objective. Improve gastrointestinal cancer recognition accuracy on endoscopy imagery.

Method. Hybrid ensemble of transformer + CNN backbones with class-aware fusion.

Result. Q1 publication; framework reusable for other GI lesion classes.

2024 · Cluster Computing Q1

Secure CBIR via Dominant Local Patterns + Watermark Encryption

Objective. Privacy-preserving content-based image retrieval over cloud.

Method. Dominant local patterns for descriptor extraction; watermark encryption for cloud storage.

Result. Maintains retrieval accuracy while enforcing confidentiality.

2023 · IEEE Access Q1

Blockchain-Assisted CP-DABE for Cloud Communication

Objective. Stronger attribute-based access control for cloud data exchange.

Method. Blockchain-assisted ciphertext-policy decentralized ABE.

Result. Practical performance with stronger trust guarantees; IEEE Access publication.
