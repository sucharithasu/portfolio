Collaboration Workflow

From discovery call to deployed prototype.

Step 01 · Week 1

Discovery & Scoping

30-min intro call · shared problem statement · success metrics · data & constraints map.

Step 02 · Weeks 2–3

Feasibility Study

Literature scan · baseline replication · feasibility memo with shortlisted approaches and risk register.

Step 03 · Weeks 4–10

Prototype Sprint

Model development · evaluation harness · weekly checkpoints with shared notebooks and metrics.

Step 04 · Weeks 11–14

Validation & Hand-off

Holdout evaluation · documentation · code hand-off · model card and deployment notes.

Step 05 · Ongoing

Publications & Patents

Joint paper or patent (IP-aware) · co-authored publications · technical reports for internal stakeholders.

Step 06 · Optional

Ongoing Advisory

Quarterly technical advisory retainer · review of internal ML roadmaps · talent pipeline from CSE/Data Science programs.
