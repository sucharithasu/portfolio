Research Domains

Six interconnected lines of inquiry.

From clinical image analysis to multimodal generative systems — work spans the AI stack with a clear translational focus on healthcare and intelligent retrieval.

Core Focus

Computer Vision & Image Retrieval

Novel local-pattern descriptors, Zernike moments and dictionary-clustering approaches for content-based image retrieval, including biomedical and texture domains.

Healthcare AI

Medical Image Analysis

CNN frameworks for brain-tumor detection on MRI, ensemble learning for GI cancer recognition, and prostate detection on MR-TRUS fusion images.

2025+ Frontier

Generative & Multimodal AI

Selective gradient diffusion for text-guided augmentation, vision transformers with intra-attention, and dynamic graph networks for cross-modal image-text retrieval.

Deep Learning

Hybrid Architectures

Vision-transformer hybrids, ensemble formers, and CNN+filter pipelines designed for high-stakes diagnostic and recognition tasks.

Edge & Cloud

Edge Intelligence & Cloud Security

Trust-and-reputation models for mobile edge networks, blockchain-assisted CP-DABE in cloud, and CBIR with dominant patterns + watermark encryption.

Blockchain + AI

Trusted Health Data Systems

Private Ethereum ledgers for organ donation, EMR on blockchain, and semantic data pricing in Web 3.0 — bridging AI with auditable infrastructure.
